Temperature

This plot displays the relationship between temperature and latitude.

As expected, the plot shows that the closer you get to the equator, the hotter the temperature is.

The plot also shows the relationship between temperature and time of year. The temperature is noticably hotter in the southern hemishere than in the northern hemisphere. This is due to the tilt of the earth at the time of year that this data was recorded.
